About Me

I'm an Integrated BS-MS graduate from IISER Kolkata with a Physics major and Computer Science minor. My research focuses on bias detection in large language models using legal texts, exploring how AI systems can be made more fair and equitable.

Currently working on my MS thesis under Prof. Kripabandhu Ghosh, where I utilize LLMs to predict Supreme Court case outcomes and measure gender bias sensitivity through linguistic interventions. My work demonstrates how LLMs internalize societal biases and how we can potentially mitigate them.

I'm passionate about the intersection of AI and social justice, developing tools that can democratize access to legal information and healthcare. My goal is to pursue PhD research in AI bias detection and fairness, contributing to more equitable AI systems.
